YouTube Timestamp Bookmarker

Auto-update bookmarks:

When enabled, your bookmarks will automatically update with the current timestamp when you pause a YouTube video.

How it works:

  1. Play any YouTube video
  2. Pause at an important moment
  3. The extension updates your bookmark with the timestamp
  4. Click the bookmark later to resume from that exact point
